    CombineMaterial

    Extends:

    * → CombineMaterial

    A material for combining two textures.

    This material supports the two blend modes Add and Screen.

    In Screen mode, the two textures are effectively projected on a white screen simultaneously. In Add mode, the textures are simply added together which often produces washed out results.

EffectComposer Class

Defined in: src\effect-composer.js:5
Module: postprocessing

The effect composer may be used in place of a normal WebGLRenderer.

The composer will disable the auto clear behaviour of the provided renderer in order to prevent unnecessary clear operations. You might want to use a RenderPass as your first pass to automatically clear the screen and render the scene to a texture for further processing.
